Subject: First appeal under the Right to Information Act, 2005

The applicant submitted an RTI application to a public authority seeking copies of inspection records and correspondence relating to a public works file. The application was submitted with proof of delivery. No reply was received within thirty days from the date of receipt by the public authority.

The applicant now seeks a direction that the available information be supplied in accordance with the RTI Act, subject to any valid exemption. The applicant also requests that the delay in response be considered by the appellate authority.

Important facts:
- RTI application filed by the applicant.
- Public authority received the request.
- No reply was given within thirty days.
- First appeal is being prepared.
- Relief sought: supply of available records and consideration of delay.
