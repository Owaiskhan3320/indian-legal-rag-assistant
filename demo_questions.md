# Demo Questions

Use one question per screenshot or demo segment.

## 1. Statute-First Constitutional Question

```text
Which constitutional provision protects personal liberty in India?
```

Expected behaviour: routes to official law and answers using Article 21.

## 2. Property / Article 300A

```text
Can a person be deprived of property without authority of law under the Constitution of India?
```

Expected behaviour: routes to official law and answers using Article 300A.

## 3. Consumer Remedy

```text
What remedy is available if an online seller refuses to refund money for a defective product?
```

Expected behaviour: routes to Consumer Protection Act remedies.

## 4. RTI Procedure

```text
What can an applicant do if an RTI application is not answered within 30 days?
```

Expected behaviour: routes law-first to RTI response/appeal provisions.

## 5. Similar Case Retrieval

```text
Are there similar cases where a government employee challenged dismissal because they were not given a reasonable opportunity of hearing?
```

Expected behaviour: retrieves similar case-law authorities and explains limits.

## 6. Case Explanation

```text
Consumer_Disputes_2009_2853 explain this case
```

Expected behaviour: explains facts, issue, decision, reasoning, and relevance from the case corpus.

## 7. Uploaded Document Q/A

After uploading a safe sample document:

```text
What is the main issue in this document?
```

Expected behaviour: answers from the uploaded document lane.
